A Brief Explanation of Colour Theory

Intro

Understanding colours is essential for many creative projects. This website will aim to give absolute beginners a little more clarity of colour theory.

In the next few pages, we'll cover the absolute basics of colour theory, from HSV to colour symbolism to common colour schemes.

Fun fact, I'm actually partially colourblind, but as we'll learn later on, this is actually not as big of a deal as you might think.
